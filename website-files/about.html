Our Mission

No one does what we do.
No one.

There is no sales prospecting tool built exclusively for staffing. Not one. Every platform out there — ZoomInfo, Apollo, Seamless — was built for everyone. Which means it was built for no one in particular, and it shows every time a staffing firm tries to use it.

myScout changes that. We built a platform from the ground up around the specific way staffing sales works — the buying signals that matter, the decision-makers that count, and the intel your team actually needs to walk into a conversation ready to win.

"Prospects for Staffing. Nothing else."

We're not trying to be all things to all industries. We're trying to be the only thing that matters to one: staffing. That focus is the whole point.

The Founder

Tiffany Scott
Founder & CEO · myScout®

Tiffany Scott spent nearly two decades in the staffing industry — rising from recruiter all the way to COO at PeopleShare, one of the country's fastest-growing staffing firms. She's been in the trenches, built sales teams, and watched firsthand what separates the firms that grow from the ones that grind.

The answer almost always came down to the same thing: bad data and wasted time. Salespeople spending hours researching prospects that had no intent to buy. Outdated contact lists. Generic tools that didn't understand what a staffing firm actually needed to know before making a call.

myScout was built to solve exactly that. Not a pivot. Not a side project. A direct response to a problem Tiffany lived for 20 years — and a tool she built because no one else was building it right.
