The Problem

Your best rep does this instinctively. The rest are guessing.

The top staffing salespeople adapt their pitch, pace, and tone to every prospect. A high-D CFO wants bullet points and ROI. A high-S HR leader wants trust and time. A high-I director wants energy and story. A high-C procurement lead wants evidence and process.

Most reps send the same email to all four. That's why generic outreach falls flat — and why one rep on your team is closing deals the others can't.
