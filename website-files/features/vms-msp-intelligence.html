The Problem

The hidden trap draining your pipeline.

Every staffing firm has lived this:

You identify a target. Research the decision-maker. Warm them up for three weeks. They take the meeting. They're interested. They loop you into procurement. And that's when you find out the account is locked behind an MSP. New vendors can't bid. Existing contracts aren't up for renewal for 18 months. Three weeks of work — evaporated.

It's the most common dead-end in staffing sales, and most prospecting tools don't even flag it.

Why Generic Tools Miss This

Traditional sales intelligence doesn't know what an MSP is.

Generic prospecting platforms are built for SaaS sales, where contract cycles don't care about supplier gatekeeping. In staffing, supplier access is a multi-billion-dollar control layer that's invisible to tools built for everyone.

That's why staffing firms keep wasting pipeline on locked accounts. The tool doesn't know what it doesn't know.
