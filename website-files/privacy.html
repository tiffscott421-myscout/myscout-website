Your privacy matters. Here's how we collect, use, and protect your information at myScout.

Your Privacy Matters

myScout® ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website, myscoutleads.com.

We may collect information you provide to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details when you contact us or request information. We may also automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, like your IP address and how you interact with our site. This helps us improve our website and services.

How We Use and May Share Your Information

We use your information to respond to your requests, provide you with information about our services, and improve your experience. We may share your information with service providers who help us operate our website and deliver our services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

You have certain rights regarding your information, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. To exercise these rights or if you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at GameOn@myScoutLeads.com.

Your Rights and Our Commitment to Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but please remember that no online transmission is completely secure. We will retain your information as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law.

By using our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time, and any changes will be posted on this page.

SMS Privacy Policy

At myScout®, we respect your privacy. When you provide us with your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to our services, including appointment reminders, account updates, and limited promotional messages.

  • Information Collection: We only collect your phone number when you voluntarily provide it through our website, forms, or direct communication.
  • Use of Information: Your phone number will only be used for communication directly related to myScout's services. We do not sell or share your number with third parties.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying "STOP." Standard message and data rates may apply.
  • Data Security: We take reasonable precautions to protect your information.
  • Contact Us: If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at GameOn@myScoutLeads.com.
