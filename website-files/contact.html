FAQ

Common questions
answered.

Is myScout really free to start?

You get 50 free credits on signup — no credit card, no auto-charge. Use them to reveal emails, pull DISC profiles, detect warm intros, and see exactly what we deliver. When they run out, you upgrade or walk. We don't trick you into staying.

How is myScout different from ZoomInfo, Apollo, or AgencyLeads?

We're not lead gen. Those tools sell SaaS-style contact scraping — 50 million random companies, cold. myScout is built only for staffing: every field, every score, every signal exists because a staffing salesperson asked for it. Staffing-specific likelihood scoring, local-first contact matching, DISC profiles baked in, and every data point tagged with its source and confidence level. They're tools for everyone. We're a weapon for staffing.

What happens to unused credits?

They roll over forever. You paid for them, you keep them. Expiring credits on paying customers is a rip-off, and we're not doing it.

Who is myScout built for?

Anyone hunting job orders — staffing sales reps, perm recruiters, agency owners, TA partners, fractional recruiters, and firm owners. Whether you place temp, perm, or both, if your job is to find companies with hiring needs and convince them to work with you, myScout is your weapon.

What is Human Scout verification?

Our Human Scouts — real people on our research team — make thousands of verification calls a week to confirm contacts, employment, and hiring activity that automated systems miss. AI gets you 80% of the way; humans close the last 20% where it matters. That's why our emails land, our numbers ring the right person, and you're not burning credits on stale data. You won't find this in ZoomInfo or Apollo.

How does myScout score a company's likelihood to use staffing?

Behind every staffing likelihood score is a cross-reference of dozens of signals — hiring patterns, vendor management activity, procurement records, growth trajectory, contractor exposure, and more — scored for strength and recency. AI handles the breadth. Human Scouts verify what matters. Nothing like this exists. Go look.

What is a Scout Request?

An on-demand deep research service. Submit a target company and our Human Scout team delivers a full intel package — decision-maker contacts, DISC profile, company background, and sales strategy — in 24 hours or 4 hours. Full credit refund if we miss the window.

Do you offer enterprise pricing?

Yes. For firms with 20+ seats, we build a custom package with custom credits, dedicated support, and volume discounts. Book a call and we'll put together something that fits your team.
