FAQ

Common questions
answered.

Is myScout really free to start?

Yes. Scout Free gives you 50 credits per month at no cost, no credit card required. You can reveal emails, pull DISC profiles, detect warm intros, and build sales strategies right away.

Who is myScout built for?

Anyone who sells at a staffing firm — solo reps, sales managers, recruiters going after job orders, and firm owners. Larger packages are built for teams and firm-wide rollouts.

How is this different from ZoomInfo or Apollo?

Those tools are built for everyone. myScout is built exclusively for staffing. The data signals, features, and credit system are all designed around how staffing sales actually works — not how SaaS sales works.

What happens to unused credits?

Solo, Team, and Partner plans include 1-month credit rollover. Free and Credit Only plans do not roll over. Annual plans also include renewal bonus credits added automatically each year.

What is a Scout Request?

An on-demand deep research service. Submit a target company and our team delivers a full intel package — decision-maker contacts, DISC profile, company background, and sales strategy — in 24 hours or 4 hours. Full credit refund if we miss the window.

Do you offer enterprise pricing?

Yes. For firms with 20+ seats, we build a custom package with custom credits, dedicated support, and volume discounts. Book a call and we'll put together something that fits your team.
